02 — About

It is not about taking pictures,
but capturing a moment forever

Born in Omdurman, Sudan — the city of poets and musicians — Amged Higazi discovered his artistic voice early. He studied at the prestigious College of Fine and Applied Art in Khartoum, where he trained under the legendary Professor Hassan Alhadi, one of Sudan's most celebrated artistic minds.

His career began at Apex Press & Publishing, working as a graphic designer and journalist photographer, covering cultural events, fashion and beauty across the Arab world. Over the years he has documented Red Bull World Championships in Oman, African Union summits, international fashion shows, and the rich tapestry of life across multiple continents.

Now based in Bochum, Germany — working alongside the AfricanTide Association — Amged brings over two decades of visual storytelling to every frame. His mission is simple: to send the beauty message of peace across the world, one photograph at a time.
