HYUNDAI
Genesis Coupe 2014

The 2014 Hyundai Genesis Coupe is edging ever closer to being a real sports car. Last year's refresh — retuned steering and suspension, stronger engines, and a new eight-speed automatic — brings it to the point where it could almost rival performance models starting at far more than its price below $27,000.

LINCOLN
MKX 2014 — Luxury Midsize SUV

The 2014 Lincoln MKX is a good option among luxury midsize SUVs. It combines a comfortable, feature-filled cabin with a cushioned, composed ride — a refined American luxury experience.

JAGUAR
F-TYPE V8 Supercharged

The Jaguar F-TYPE V8 — powered by a supercharged 3.0-litre V6 in 340hp and 380hp outputs, and a heart-pounding 495hp 5.0-litre supercharged V8. A driver-focused interior blending classic sports car feel with modern technology.
